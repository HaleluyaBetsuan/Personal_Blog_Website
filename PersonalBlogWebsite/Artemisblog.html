Artemis

Description:

Artemis is an excited and active character, who loves to play around and create a friendly environment! He extremely supportive of the people in his circle and will do anything if it means keeping them safe. He is not very smart but he is really strong and is quick to understand after it's thoroughly explained. Although he looks scary, anyone who know him will tell you he is a big bear!
